During my freshman year, I started a non-profit organization called 'Padho India' (Read India) in an attempt to reduce the illiteracy rates in India. While running this NGO, I realized that there exists a huge gap between the schools that are in need of financial aid and the individuals who are willing to donate. Hence, as a part of EPICS coursework, I decided to build an application that empowers public school teachers from across the country to request much-needed materials for their students and integrated this application with our NGO site to connect donors.
